OUR OBJECTIVE
The basis of the project is research ideation and realization of the art projects that improve the lives of the people who live there in the areas far from the cosmopolitan urban centers, creating an environment that improves the lives of the citizens who live there in all senses, attracting attention to the international level bringing a flow of the people as tourists, admirers of art, design and architecture in consequence bringing in itself an economic development to support this flow of the people who follow the art that is born there. The idea is to create a great platform at the international level for pure expression of art with the internationally renowned artists or current art themes for the current world that in itself bring people to visit the iconic places
